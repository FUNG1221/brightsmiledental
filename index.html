Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to some of the most common questions from our patients.

How often should I visit the dentist? ▼
Most patients should schedule a professional dental exam and cleaning every six months. Your dentist may recommend more frequent visits depending on your oral health needs and risk factors.
Do you accept dental insurance? ▼
Yes. We work with many major dental insurance providers and can assist you in understanding your coverage and benefits. We also offer flexible payment plans for uninsured patients.
What should I do during a dental emergency? ▼
Contact our office immediately at (555) 123-4567. We offer emergency dental appointments and will provide guidance until you can be seen by our team. If after hours, our voicemail will provide emergency contact instructions.
Are dental implants a permanent solution? ▼
Dental implants are designed to be a long-term tooth replacement option. With proper care and regular checkups, they can last for many years, often 10-15 years or more. Success rates are very high when patients follow proper care instructions.
Do you offer cosmetic smile makeovers? ▼
Absolutely. We provide veneers, teeth whitening, bonding, and customized smile design solutions tailored to your goals. Schedule a consultation to discuss your smile transformation.
How long does a dental implant procedure take? ▼
The implant placement typically takes 1-2 hours. However, the complete process includes healing time (3-6 months) before the permanent crown is placed. We'll provide a detailed timeline during your consultation.
