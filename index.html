Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers about appointments, insurance, treatment options, emergency care, and your first visit.

Most patients should visit the dentist every six months for a professional exam and cleaning. Some patients may need more frequent visits depending on gum health, dental history, or ongoing treatment.

Call our office as soon as possible. We offer same-day emergency visits when available for tooth pain, broken teeth, swelling, injuries, and urgent dental concerns.

Professional teeth whitening is safe when performed under dental supervision. We evaluate your teeth and gums first to recommend the best whitening option for your smile.
